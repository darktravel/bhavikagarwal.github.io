About

Hello! I am a thesis-track M.S. student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Casey Overby Taylor. I also work as a Machine Learning Research Scientist at MasterControl. Previously, I was a research mentee at Google DeepMind and collaborated with Microsoft Research. I also worked at BlueStacks and SAP Labs after obtaining my B.Tech. from IIIT Delhi.

My primary research focus is centered around dependable Small Language Models (SLMs) and Multi-Agent Systems. I am particularly interested in retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), knowledge-graph–augmented retrieval, and parse-safe structured generation.

I am passionate about building tool-using models with strong guarantees. My research aims to create systems that can reliably ground answers in verifiable evidence, ensuring they can be trusted in safety-critical workflows.

I am always open to collaboration and mentoring students interested in NLP. If you are interested in trustworthy AI, Multi-Agent systems, or discussing application strategies, please feel free to reach out!
